FC Bayern Munich

FC Bayern Munich, founded in 1900, is Germany’s most successful football club. Known for its dominance in the Bundesliga and its strength in European competitions, Bayern is a symbol of German football excellence.

Achievements

  • 6 UEFA Champions League titles
  • 33 Bundesliga titles
  • 20 DFB-Pokal trophies
  • 2 FIFA Club World Cups

Famous Players

Franz Beckenbauer, Gerd Müller, Manuel Neuer, Thomas Müller, Robert Lewandowski
